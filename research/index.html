Research

Research interests

  • Robotics: micro-nano robots, mechanism design, robot calibration, parallel robots, continuum robots
  • Automation: micro-nano manipulation, microassembly, distributed manipulation
  • Computer vision: photogrammetry, pose estimation, visual servoing, microscopy

See my publications page for more details.

Research projects

  • TIRREX (ANR Equipex+ funding, 2021-2029): Technological Infrastructure for Robotics Research of Excellence
  • HOLO-CONTROL (ANR funding, 2021-2025): Extended machine vision control capabilities using digital holography & artificial neural networks
  • µSPIDER (ANR funding, 2019-2023): Parallel microrobot with large workspace.
  • HoloNet (ISITE BFC funding, 2020-2023): Toward extended visual control capabilities using digital holography.
  • AI-Control-Net (regional funding, 2018-2021): Neural networks applied to microropbotics and photonics.
  • Nano6D (regional funding, 2018-2021): 3D metrology at nanoscale.
  • ROBOTEX (ANR Equipex funding, 2011-2020): National network of robotics platforms
  • Robotic Nanofactory (I-SITE BFC funding, 2017-2021): Characterization and micro-assembly of micro/nanosystems below 10µm
  • COLAMIR (ANR funding, 2016-2020): Collaborative agile miniaturized robotics for ultra-precise assembly
  • NEMRO (ANR funding, 2015-2018): Microrobotic nasal endoscopy by OCT.
  • FRANCHIR (regional funding, 2014-2017): FRANche-Comté HIgh precision micro-Robotics.
  • Smart Blocks (ANR funding, 2011-2015): modular and reconfigurable surface composed of MEMS for the rapid transport of small and fragile objects.
  • Smart Surface (ANR funding, 2007-2010): MEMS surfaces able to manipulate, convey, and sort small planar objects using aerodynamic transport.
